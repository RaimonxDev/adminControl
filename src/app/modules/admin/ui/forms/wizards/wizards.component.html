Form Wizards

Form wizard examples in this page uses mat-horizontal-stepper and mat-vertical-stepper components from Angular Material. You can get more information about them over here: https://material.angular.io/components/stepper/overview.

We prepared couple examples to get you started, but these examples do not represent what you can do with stepper components. There are lots of options to them that you can configure, please take a look at the official guide to learn everything about them.
